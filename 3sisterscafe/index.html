About us

Coffee passion, coffee alternatives, latte art, warm welcome to guests

We are a modern café located in the city center of Ostrava. We offer specialty coffee made with Naughty Dog beans. We love to share our coffee stories with you, our dear guests, and also learn new ways of making coffee. We want you to feel welcome. In case of any feedback, do not hesitate to contact us.
