How did I get here? When did my love for coffee begin? I actually remember the times when I considered the taste of coffee to be awful. Then I added a lot of sugar and milk to my coffee at some point... aaand it tasted better. It was a long time ago so I cannot recall the details but I think it was only after I have tasted specialty coffee when realized that I do no need sugar in it. I am still working on being able to resist having milk in my cup. My love for coffee has been growing gradually for the past few year and now I am kind of coffee addict. I don't need my coffee every day. But I need good quality coffee in my life! I am in France now and I do not have any coffee equipment here with me so the only option is to visit cafés. And that's an activity that really makes me smile :) It actualy is one my favorite activities. This is a list of my favorite activities now:

  1. hiking or walking in the nature (parcs, forests)
  2. walking around historical cities
  3. visiting cafés
  4. experimenting with vegan ingredients - cooking for my husband
  5. jogging maybe?
